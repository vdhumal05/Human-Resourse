1)Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Human Resources (HR) data to identify employee attrition patterns, workforce trends, and key organizational insights. The goal is to use data-driven techniques to understand factors influencing employee retention and performance.

2)problem Statement

To analyze HR data and answer key business questions such as:

What factors influence employee attrition?

Which departments show higher turnover?

How do salary, job role, and experience affect retention?

What patterns exist in employee demographics?
